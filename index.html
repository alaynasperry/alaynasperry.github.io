THE PHOTOGRAPHER
Hi I'm Layna!
I've always had a love for photography and finding unique ways to capture life's little moments.

My passion stemmed from the typical pretty sunsets and flowers that grew in my mothers garden. I took my first photography class at 15 and ever sunce, have persistently worked to grow my skills.

I take photos to capture those little moments that might not last forever in your mind. To have something that you can always rely on, emphasizing every little detail. Our world is such a beautiful place full of beautiful moments, and I make it my job to bring that forward through my work.
